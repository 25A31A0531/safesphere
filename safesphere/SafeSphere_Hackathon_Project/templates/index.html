About SafeSphere

SafeSphere is a fully AI-powered system that works anywhere without user interaction and can save lives by detecting emergencies in real-time. Whether on the road, at home, or in public spaces, the AI constantly monitors for signs of danger, ensuring help arrives instantly.

Universal Features

Multi-modal AI detection algorithms actively safeguarding your environment.

AI Accident Detection

Monitors motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) to instantly detect vehicular or personal accidents.

AI Fire Detection

Computer vision analyzes camera feeds for flames and smoke patterns to detect fire outbreaks early.

Panic & Crowd Detection

Identifies panic situations and sudden crowd dispersion indicating dangerous events unfolding.

Voice Emergency

Listens for specific voice commands and shouts like "Help!" or "Fire!" using natural language processing.

Works Everywhere

Adaptable for roads, homes, hospitals, malls, airports, railway stations, and tourist places.

Camera AI

Our advanced computer vision models actively scan environments using available cameras to detect visual anomalies.

It can precisely identify fire outbreaks, accidents, and panic situations in real-time without relying on external triggers. SafeSphere analyzes footage purely mathematically, maintaining zero-trust privacy.

Works Without Camera

Privacy matters. Or your phone might simply be in your pocket. SafeSphere utilizes backup detection methods completely independent of cameras:

  • Accelerometer: Sudden impact analysis.
  • Gyroscope: Unusual rotational changes or flips.
  • GPS: Sudden drastic speed drops indicating collision.
  • No Movement Detection: Prolonged immobility post-impact.

Severity Detection

Intelligently classifies the magnitude of emergencies to dispatch appropriate level of help.

Accidents

Categorizes crashes into Minor, Moderate, and Severe based on g-force metrics and deformation impact.

Fires

Assesses fire scale (Small, Medium, Large) from pixel spread and volumetric smoke density.

Panic Levels

Identifies panic intensities based on audio decibels and rapid, erratic crowd motion vectors.

Smart Countdown System

To maintain user control, the system initiates a countdown before dispatching external alerts, featuring a standard Cancel Option to prevent false alarms.

Adaptive Timing: Default 10-second countdown dynamically shifts depending on context (Fire 5 sec, Panic 8 sec, Accident 10 sec).

Quick Trigger Feature

For high-severity crashes, the system instantly sends an alert packet before the phone undergoes a potential hardware shutdown, bypassing standard countdowns.

Smart Shutdown Analysis

A specialized deep-learning sub-module precisely analyzes pre-shutdown conditions to differentiate real accidents that destroy devices from intentional manual shutdowns.

Offline Mode

  • Detection works fully offline on-device.
  • Stores data safely.
  • Sends pending alerts immediately when network returns.
  • Local alarm activation (Max volume sound + Flashlight stroboscope).

Emergency Response Protocol

When the AI decides action is required, SafeSphere executes multiple protocols in milliseconds.

Auto Call

Automatically triggers a VoIP or cellular call to universal emergency numbers (e.g. 112).

Live SMS

Dispatches text messages containing live GPS tracking links to dispatchers.

Contact Alerts

Notifies pre-configured family members and specific emergency contacts instantly.

Smartwatch Integration

Optional but powerful. Connects to wearables for real-time biometric tracking:

  • Heart rate monitoring and Oxygen level (SpO2).
  • Silent Heart attack detection.
  • Dramatically improves severity detection accuracy.

Health & Injury Analysis

By synchronizing physical accident sensor data (impact vectors) with biometric health streams, the AI estimates injury severity and calculates possible blood loss for advancing paramedics.

Public Safety

SafeSphere scales up. In public places, it handles crowd monitoring and interfaces with public digital infrastructure to provide smart evacuation guidance towards the nearest unblocked exit.

False Alarm Prevention

  • Multi-sensor Validation: Hardware corroboration.
  • AI Decision System: Weighing context.
  • User Confirmation: Countdown prompts.
  • Severity Filtering: Removing noise.
